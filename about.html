About NWDOH Pharmaceutical and Surgical Stock Status

Efficient stock management is crucial in ensuring continuous healthcare services. The NWDOH Pharmaceutical and Surgical Stock Status platform was developed to provide real-time insights into stock levels across hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies in South Africa. This initiative enhances supply chain transparency, reduces shortages, and ensures that essential medicines and surgical items are always available where they are needed most.

Why This Application Was Created

The healthcare sector faces significant challenges in managing medical stock effectively. The primary objectives behind this application include:

  • Real-Time Stock Monitoring – Ensuring hospitals and pharmacies have accurate data on medicine and surgical stock availability.
  • Improved Supplier Coordination – Providing suppliers with up-to-date demand trends to optimize distribution.
  • Minimized Shortages & Wastage – Streamlining procurement processes to prevent shortages while reducing excess stock wastage.
  • Enhanced Communication – Enabling healthcare professionals to share critical updates and documents efficiently.

This platform was developed to empower pharmacists, healthcare managers, and suppliers with the right tools to maintain an uninterrupted flow of essential medical supplies, ultimately contributing to better patient care.
